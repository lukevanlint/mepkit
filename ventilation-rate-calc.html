Method

Dwelling: use the higher of bedroom-based minimum rate and 0.3 L/s per m² of internal floor area.

Non-dwelling office: use the higher of 10 L/s per person or 1 L/s per m².

Occupancy method: Ventilation Rate = Occupants × L/s per person.

ACH method: Ventilation Rate (m³/h) = Room Volume × ACH.

Conversion: Ventilation Rate (L/s) = m³/h ÷ 3.6.

Reference

This calculator provides an indicative ventilation rate based on the selected building type and calculation route.

Dwelling route: Approved Document F Volume 1 applies to dwellings. Whole-dwelling ventilation is commonly checked as the higher of a bedroom-based minimum rate and 0.3 L/s per m² of internal floor area.

Non-dwelling route: Approved Document F Volume 2 applies to buildings other than dwellings. For offices, outdoor air should be provided at the higher of 10 L/s per person or 1 L/s per m².

School route: school ventilation should be checked against BB 101 rather than treated as a generic non-dwelling preset.

Scope note: this calculator is an early-stage estimating tool. Final requirements may also depend on building use, pollutant loads, extract strategy, ventilation effectiveness, thermal comfort, project-specific criteria and the agreed compliance strategy with the design team and building control body.
