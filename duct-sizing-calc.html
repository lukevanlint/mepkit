Reference

This calculator provides an indicative duct size or duct check from airflow and velocity.

Key relationships used:

Area = Flow ÷ Velocity

Circular Diameter = √(4A ÷ π)

Rectangular Area = Width × Height

Velocity Pressure = 0.5 × ρ × v²

An air density of 1.2 kg/m³ is assumed for the velocity pressure check.

Duct sizing and ductwork design are commonly checked against the CIBSE ductwork guidance family, including Guide B2: Ventilation and ductwork and Guide C: Reference data. In practice, engineers also commonly use Guide C4 style duct sizing data and methods for sizing and checking airflow, velocity and pressure effects. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

The velocity presets in this calculator are indicative design conventions only. They are intended as practical starting points rather than fixed regulatory limits. Final design should be checked against project-specific noise criteria, pressure drop allowances and coordination constraints. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Regulatory note: ventilation compliance in England is generally demonstrated against Approved Document F, which is split into Volume 1 for dwellings and Volume 2 for buildings other than dwellings. This calculator does not itself demonstrate Part F compliance. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Scope note: this calculator is an indicative sizing tool only. Final design should also consider pressure drop, noise criteria, aspect ratio limits, available space, fan selection, balancing and project-specific ductwork standards.
