Method

Area = Flow ÷ Velocity

Circular Diameter = √(4A ÷ π)

Velocity Pressure = 0.5 × ρ × v²

Reference

An air density of 1.2 kg/m³ is assumed for velocity pressure checks.

Duct sizing is commonly undertaken using guidance contained within CIBSE Guide B2: Ventilation and ductwork, CIBSE Guide C: Reference data, and sizing methods presented in CIBSE Guide C4: Flow of fluids in pipes and ducts.

The velocity presets in this calculator represent indicative design conventions only used in practice to balance duct size, noise and pressure drop.

Regulatory note: ventilation compliance in England is typically demonstrated against Approved Document F, which is divided into Volume 1 (dwellings) and Volume 2 (buildings other than dwellings).

Scope note: this calculator provides an indicative duct size only. Final design should consider noise criteria, pressure drop, space constraints, fan selection and ductwork standards.
