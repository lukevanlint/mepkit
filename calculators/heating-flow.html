Reference

This calculator uses the standard heating water relationship: Flow Rate (L/s) = Heat Load ÷ (4.18 × ΔT). The constant 4.18 represents the specific heat capacity of water at typical heating system temperatures. This relationship is widely used in building services engineering to estimate water flow rates for low temperature hot water systems.

Scope note: this calculator determines flow rate only. Final system design should also consider pipe velocity, pressure drop, pump head, controls strategy and project-specific design requirements.
