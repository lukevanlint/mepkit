Method

Specific fan power is calculated from total fan input power and system airflow:

SFP = P ÷ Q

Where fan power is not known, it can be estimated from airflow, total pressure and overall efficiency:

P = (Q × ΔP) ÷ η
  • SFP = specific fan power
  • P = total fan input power (W)
  • Q = airflow rate (L/s or m³/s depending on expression)
  • ΔP = total system pressure (Pa)
  • η = overall fan system efficiency

Reference
