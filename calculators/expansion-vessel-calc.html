Method

Reference

This calculator estimates the required nominal expansion vessel volume from system water content, estimated thermal expansion and the working pressure range of the system.

Key relationships used:

Expansion Volume = System Volume × Expansion Factor

Reserve Volume = max(0.5% of System Volume, 3L)

Acceptance Factor = (Pmax - Pmin) ÷ (Pmax + 1)

Minimum Vessel Size = Total Acceptance Volume ÷ Acceptance Factor

CIBSE guidance notes that expansion vessel sizing should be carried out in accordance with BS EN 12828, with worked examples available in CIBSE AM14.

CIBSE also notes that vessels greater than 15 litres should include a reserve of at least 0.5% of total system water content, with a minimum of 3 litres.

Scope note: this calculator is an indicative estimating tool. In auto mode, the expansion factor is estimated from water density change between fill temperature and maximum temperature. Final design should confirm the correct system pressures, operating temperatures, reserve allowance and selected vessel against project requirements and manufacturer data.
