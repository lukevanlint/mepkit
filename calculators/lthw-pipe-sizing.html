Method

Pipe diameter is derived from the standard flow and velocity relationship:

Q = A × v

Rearranged for circular pipe diameter:

d = √(4Q / πv)
  • Q = volumetric flow rate (m³/s)
  • A = pipe cross-sectional area (m²)
  • v = design velocity (m/s)
