Reference

This calculator uses the standard heating water relationship:

Flow Rate (L/s) = Heat Load ÷ (4.18 × ΔT)

The calculated flow rate is then compared against standard pipe sizes to identify the smallest pipe that maintains velocity within the selected limit.

Note: copper and steel pipe systems use different standard size series, so the recommended pipe size depends on the selected material.

Velocity guidance: commentary shown in the result reflects traditional accepted design norms described in CIBSE guidance for closed water systems rather than fixed absolute limits.

Scope note: sizing is based on velocity only. Final pipe selection should also consider pressure drop, pump head, noise, erosion risk, manufacturer data and project standards.
