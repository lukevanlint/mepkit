System Water Volume Estimator

Estimate heating system water volume from pipework, emitters and plant allowances.

Use this estimator for preliminary sealed heating system checks where a full detailed water content schedule is not yet available. It combines pipework water content with simple allowances for emitters and plant to provide an indicative total system volume.

Method

Total system volume is estimated by summing pipework content, emitter allowances and plant allowances:

Vₜ = Vₚ + Vₑ + Vₚₗ

Pipework volume is estimated from length and typical water content per metre:

Vₚ = Σ (Pipe Length × Water Content per metre)

  • Vₚ = total pipework water volume (L)

Reference

  • This estimator is intended for preliminary system water content checks where a detailed schedule is not yet available.
  • Pipework content is based on typical internal water volumes for common heating pipe sizes.
  • Emitter and plant allowances should be refined using project equipment schedules and manufacturer data where available.
  • Where applicable, final design should align with project specifications, manufacturer requirements and associated vessel / pressurisation calculations.
Design note: This estimator provides indicative system water volume only. Final expansion vessel sizing and pressurisation design should be based on confirmed plant content, terminal content, detailed pipework lengths and manufacturer data.
