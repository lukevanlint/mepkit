Method

Single-phase power is calculated from:

P = V × I × PF × η

Three-phase power is calculated from:

P = √3 × V × I × PF × η
  • P = power (W)
  • V = voltage (V)
  • I = current (A)
  • PF = power factor
  • η = efficiency

Reference
