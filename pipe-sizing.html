Reference

This calculator uses the standard LTHW water flow relationship:

Flow Rate (L/s) = Heat Load ÷ (4.18 × ΔT)

It then estimates the minimum pipe internal diameter required to keep water velocity within the selected limit.

Pipe sizes are indicative only and should be checked against final pressure drop, noise criteria, manufacturer data and project standards.
